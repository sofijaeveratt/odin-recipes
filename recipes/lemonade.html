Lemonade

TThis lemonade is great on hot days to cool down

Prep

10 mins

Servings

10

Ingredients

  • 6 lemons, juiced
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes

Steps

  1. In a large pitches combine lemon juice and water.
  2. Stir in sugar until it dissolves, then add the ice cubes.
